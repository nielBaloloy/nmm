MULADBUCAD BEACH RESORT


Muladbucad is one of the fewest white beaches in Albay accessible by land. An hour away from Legazpi, this place is where you feel the scent of nature, hear the songs of wild birds and the relaxing whispers of the waves. Muladbucad is being run and managed by the heirs of Simplicio Dawal. These warm and hospitable people make it sure that everybody feels like home.


Muladbucad, Manito, Albay
