Cagraray Eco-Energy Park: Hilltop park in Albay


Cagraray Eco-Energy Park is an ecological park that features trekking, zip lines, a swimming pool, and an open-air amphitheater overlooking the picturesque Albay Gulf. Roughly an hour away from Legazpi City, Cagraray Island, where the eco-park is situated, is also known for the renowned Misibis Bay

Bacacay, Albay

How to get to Cagraray?


It could get really hot since there is no shade available when taking the tour.
Wear a cap or use an umbrella. Bring a bottle of water and a fan with you.
Zipline, hanging bridge and trekking are other activities one may try while in the eco-park.
The fee is ₱25 per adult and ₱10 per child. The park is open from 8am to 5pm, Monday to Sunday.
