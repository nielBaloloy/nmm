How to get to Pinamuntugan Island?


If you're coming from Manila, Quezon, CamNorte, CamSur, the easiest way to get to Pinamuntugan is by riding a bus en route to Tabaco City. After reaching Tabaco, follow the guide above "Via Tabaco City".
If you're coming from Sorsogon, ride a bus going to Tabaco City and tell the operator to drop you off at Bacacay. After reaching Bacacay, follow the guide above "Bacacay - Bacacay Port".
If you're a resident of Albay, you can either ride a bus or van going to Tabaco or Bacacay. If you choose to ride via Tabaco, tell the operator to drop you at Bacacay. After reaching Bacacay, follow the guide above "Bacacay - Bacacay Port".
