Farmplate:Relax in a farm setting in Albay


Imagine yourself relaxing in a farm, free from the hustle and bustle of city life even for just a few hours. Or set up tent to camp overnight in a peaceful and secured location. Then Farm Plate is for you. Walk around the farm to see the big pigeon houses, the red cottage with a windmill water pump, the stone house, the camp site, the horses, the flower beds. Sit in one of the swings and cottages and just enjoy the view of the Mayon Volcano while sipping on fresh coconut juice. Kids can have a good time at the playground or enjoy the carabao ride or just bike around the farm. It is a good way to enjoy the outdoors and relax at the same time.

Gabawan Daraga, Albay

RATINGS

How to get to Farmplate?


From Caltex, Daraga, you can ride on a tricycle going to Gabawan, Daraga, and tell the driver to get you to FarmPlate. Fare is 20 pesos per pax.
Entrance fee is P150.00 per pax for adults and P100.00 per pax for kids (12 years old below), as of June 2019.
